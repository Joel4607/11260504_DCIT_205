Projects.

E-gate systems.

Typically, RFID (radio frequency identification) technology is used by e-gate systems. Staff and student ID cards have incorporated RFID tags. A reader scans the RFID tag when a student or staff member approaches an e-gate. The gate will open if the RFID tag is legitimate.

Miniature Robotic Car.

Students will design, construct, and program a tiny robotic car that can travel a route as part of this project. They will work in groups. There will be a variety of challenges in the course, including curves, ramps, and obstacles. The robot must be able to successfully travel the course and avoid obstacles.
